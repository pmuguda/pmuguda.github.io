About Me

Hi, I'm Pavan Muguda Sanjeevamurthy, an Earth observation and remote sensing specialist with a Master's degree in Earth Oriented Space Science Technology from the Technical University of Munich. With over three years of professional experience, I have worked on various projects involving algorithm development, data processing pipelines, and machine learning techniques for diverse Earth observation applications.

Currently, I am a Project Assistant at the Technical University of Wien, where I focus on the development of drought indicators workflow and quality assurance of ASCAT products. I am passionate about using my skills to contribute to the forefront of remote sensing innovation.

My expertise includes Python, MATLAB, IDL, C, Julia, and various GIS software and tools such as Google Earth Engine, ESA SNAP, ArcGIS, and QGIS. I am proficient in multiple programming languages and have a strong foundation in project management using Git, CI/CD, and Docker.
